ABOUT AFFORD MEDICO

Afford Medico helps NGOs to reach out to thousands of prospective donors with their wonderful work and beautiful causes. This unique platform empowers small and medium sized NGOs create their digital presence. By providing ratings from the donors who have made a donation to their favourite cause and the loved charity, AffordMedico provides the unique platform where a donor can give with confidence.

Afford Medico believes in the power of empowerment through correct information. We let our donors evaluate and rate the NGOs based upon their experience. Every donor is given the choice to rate their experience with the NGO, when completing their donation. These ratings act as the guidelines for other prospective donors, when they are considering a donation. Every Indian charity is welcome to register on Donatio and use this platform to reach out to the donors across the country. Instead of we are deciding, whether the given NGO is credible or not, we let the donor decide based upon their giving experience.
